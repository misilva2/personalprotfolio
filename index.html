Identity

About Me

I am Micka Silva, also known as Money Meeks. I am currently a biomedical science student at Arizona State University, with plans to continue toward becoming a physician assistant.

I am a builder of worlds, some made of code and others made of rhythm and rhyme. With a background in anthropology and a passion for web development, I see life through a lens of systems and storytelling. My creative process is fueled by poetry and songwriting, often inspired by the cinematic landscapes of the Southwest.

Life is not all data and stanzas, though. I am just as likely to be deep in a game universe as I am out in the mud on four-wheelers. My perfect weekend balances a morning of fishing or hiking with an evening at the desk, building something new for the web.

I am a co-founder of Southern Arizona Marketing Systems, where I help local businesses grow through strategy, branding, and execution.

I also work alongside my dad and aunt at The Arctic Circle in Bisbee, AZ. During one stretch with me leading operations, we sold out two weekends in a row and grossed over $10,000 in three weeks.
