Identity

About Me

I am Micka Silva, also known as Money Meeks. I am a student at Arizona State University pursuing a degree in biomedical science, and I plan to continue my education to become a physician assistant.

I am a builder of worlds, some made of code and others made of rhythm and rhyme. With a background in anthropology and a passion for web development, I look at life through a lens of systems and storytelling. My creative process is fueled by poetry and songwriting, often inspired by the cinematic landscapes of the Southwest.

Life is not all data and stanzas, though. I am just as likely to be deep in a video game universe as I am out in the mud on four-wheelers. My perfect weekend balances a morning of fishing or hiking with an evening at the desk, building something new for the web.

I am an owner and co-founder of Southern Arizona Marketing Systems, where I help local businesses grow through strategy, branding, and execution.

I work alongside my dad and aunt, who are owners of The Arctic Circle in Bisbee, AZ. With me in charge, The Arctic sold out two weekends in a row and grossed over $10,000 in three weeks.
